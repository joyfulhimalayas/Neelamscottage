Overview

Welcome to the Neelams' Countryside Cottage ! On this fine riverside property - bordered by the Apple Valley Resort and the National Highway, we at Neelams' bring the feeling of homeliness and safety a step closer to you. Keeping up with all modern standards of sanitation and hygiene, this place is like no other. Countryside Cottage is a stone and all wood house built on the traditional Kath-Kuni Design, with a glazed verandah, giving you a full view of the Lawn and the River Beas.

Going around is pretty convenient. There is a bus stop and an auto-rickshaw stand 200 Yards away from the cottage. If you have your own car, then driving to the town will ideally take you ten minutes. Mohal is 45 minutes away from Manali and Kasol, the two main tourist attractions. There are numerous treks and hikes in and around Mohal. We are located 5 kms from the domestic airport and also provide pick-n-drop facility.

We are available for your assistance 24x7. We can guarantee that you'll be looked after very well and it would be our great pleasure and privilege to make your stay joyous and comfortable.
